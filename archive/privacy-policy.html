1. About This Policy

Qamar Construction LLC ("Qamar Construction," "we," "our," or "us"), located at 12650 Crossroads Park Dr, Suite 125, Houston TX 77065, operates the website qamarconstruction.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or contact us about our construction services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, and business address when you fill out our contact or quote request forms.
  • Project information: details about your construction project, timeline, budget, and property location that you share with us.
  • Communications: records of correspondence you send us via email, phone, WhatsApp, or our website forms.

We may also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring website, through standard web server logs and analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide project estimates

  • Send you information about our services that may be relevant to your needs
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors use it
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and business (such as email service providers or CRM software), under strict confidentiality obligations. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Qamar Construction, our clients, or others.

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather analytics data.
