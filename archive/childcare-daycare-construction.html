DFPS COMPLIANCE

We Know DFPS Requirements Inside and Out

The Texas Department of Family and Protective Services sets strict physical facility standards for licensed childcare centers. Building to those standards from day one — not retrofitting after the fact — is what we do.

📐 Indoor Space Requirements

DFPS requires a minimum of 35 square feet of usable indoor activity space per child in the licensed capacity. This space excludes bathrooms, hallways, storage, and areas not used for child activity. We design your classroom layout to maximize licensed capacity within your footprint.

🌳 Outdoor Play Area Requirements

Licensed centers must provide 80 square feet of outdoor play space per child for the number of children using the play area at one time. Play areas must be fenced, graded for drainage, and equipped with age-appropriate surface materials. We design, grade, and build compliant play yards.

🚽 Toilet & Handwashing Ratios

Texas requires one toilet and handwashing sink per 15 children ages 2 and older, and one toilet per 10 children under age 2. Infant rooms require diaper change stations with sinks within arm's reach. We calculate and build restroom configurations to support your desired licensed capacity.

🚪 Exit Access & Life Safety

Every classroom must have direct exit access to the outside or to a corridor with exterior exit. Fire marshal requirements include sprinkler systems for larger facilities, smoke detectors in every room, fire-rated corridors, and emergency lighting. We coordinate fire marshal plan review as part of our scope.

🔒 Security & Entry Control

Best-practice childcare design requires controlled entry vestibules with intercom, camera, and remotely released door hardware so staff can screen visitors before granting access. All play yard fencing must have self-latching, child-proof gates. We build security infrastructure into every childcare project.

🍽️ Kitchen & Food Service

Centers that prepare or serve food must meet Harris County Health Department food service facility requirements. This includes commercial kitchen equipment, three-compartment sinks, handwashing sinks, hood ventilation, grease trap, and proper refrigeration. We build kitchens that pass both DFPS and health department inspection.
