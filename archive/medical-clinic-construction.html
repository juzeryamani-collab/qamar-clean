Medical Clinic Construction Isn't Standard Commercial Work

Medical clinic construction involves layers of regulatory compliance, clinical workflow design, and technical MEP requirements that standard commercial contractors are not equipped to handle. Getting it wrong means failed inspections, costly rework, delayed openings — or worse, compliance violations after you open.

Qamar Construction brings 25+ years of healthcare construction expertise. We have built primary care clinics, urgent care centers, specialty practices, and multi-physician group facilities across Greater Houston. We know what TDLR requires, how to design for HIPAA compliance, and how to build infection-control environments that pass final inspection the first time.

Medical Clinic Construction Costs in Houston

Medical clinic construction costs vary significantly based on size, specialty type, and whether you're building from shell or renovating existing space. These ranges reflect real projects in the Houston market in 2024–2026.

Healthcare MEP systems — medical-grade HVAC, plumbing in every exam room, electrical for diagnostic equipment — are the primary cost driver above standard commercial construction. Renovating existing space typically costs 30–50% less than new shell construction.

💡 Budget Tip
Second-generation medical spaces (previously occupied clinics) can significantly reduce your construction cost vs. raw shell build-outs — we can assess any space you're considering and give you a real number before you sign a lease.

Regulatory Expertise

Medical Clinic Permits in Texas:
We Handle Every Step

Healthcare construction in Texas involves multiple regulatory bodies. We've navigated this process dozens of times. You don't have to.

🏛️

City of Houston Building Permits

Commercial building permits for healthcare use, including mechanical, electrical, plumbing sub-permits. We submit, track, and respond to all RFIs on your behalf.

📜

TDLR Healthcare Review

Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation review for qualifying healthcare facilities. We prepare compliant construction documents and manage the state review process.

🔥

Fire Marshal Inspections

Fire suppression, egress, emergency lighting, and occupancy load compliance for healthcare use. We coordinate all fire marshal inspections and address any deficiencies immediately.

♿

ADA Compliance Documentation

Full ADA compliance for all patient-accessible spaces — not just to meet code, but to provide genuinely accessible care environments for your patients.

✅

Certificate of Occupancy

We manage the final CO inspection process for healthcare occupancy classification — coordinating all trades and documentation so you receive your CO before your first patient appointment.

🔒

Infection Control Compliance

ICRA protocols during phased renovations, construction barrier management, and infection control documentation — all compliant with The Joint Commission standards for construction in healthcare environments.
