FAQ

Retail Construction Questions Answered

How much does retail store construction cost in Houston?
Retail storefront construction in Houston ranges from $75/sq ft for basic tenant improvements to $220/sq ft for high-end boutique or flagship builds. A typical 1,500 sq ft mid-level retail space runs $150,000–$210,000. Food retail and specialty stores with plumbing and refrigeration typically fall in the $150–$200/sq ft range.
How long does retail build-out take in Houston?
A standard retail tenant improvement in Houston takes 8–14 weeks from permit approval to certificate of occupancy. Permitting takes 3–6 weeks. High-finish boutique builds or stores requiring significant plumbing or structural work may take 14–20 weeks. We work backward from your opening date and build a schedule to hit it.
Do you work with franchise design packages?
Yes. We have extensive experience working within franchisor design standards, approved vendor lists, and construction approval processes. We coordinate with corporate architects, submit required documentation for franchise construction approvals, and build to the standards required for your franchise agreement.
Do you handle landlord TI coordination?
Yes. We work directly with your landlord or property management company to coordinate tenant improvement allowances, submit required plans for landlord approval, and ensure our work complies with building rules and base building systems. We have experience with all major Houston commercial landlords and shopping center requirements.
