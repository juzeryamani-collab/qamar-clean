Our Story

Built on a Foundation of Trust, Quality, and Accountability

Qamar Construction was founded in Houston in 1999 with a single guiding principle: build every project as if it were our own. Over the past 25+ years, that principle has never wavered — and it's why our clients come back, and why they refer their colleagues, partners, and friends.

We started with residential remodels and commercial tenant improvements. As our reputation grew, healthcare construction became a major focus — and today, Qamar is one of Houston's go-to contractors for dental clinic construction, medical clinic build-outs, and specialty healthcare facilities across the region.

What sets us apart isn't just the quality of our construction — it's how we run a project. We operate as a true design-build partner, managing everything from permits and design to final punch-list and certificate of occupancy. Our clients never have to coordinate between an architect, a permit runner, and a GC. We handle it all.

1999
Founded in Houston, TX. First projects: residential remodels and commercial tenant improvements in the northwest Houston corridor.
2006
Completed first dental clinic build-out. Recognized the unique demands of healthcare construction and began specializing.
2012
Reached 50+ completed projects. Expanded service area to cover all of Greater Houston — 70+ cities across Harris, Fort Bend, Brazoria, Galveston and Montgomery counties.
2018
Formalized turn-key design-build offering. Clients now get in-house architectural coordination, permitting management, and construction — single point of accountability.
2024
100+ completed projects. 5-star Google rating. Continued BBB Accreditation. One of Houston's most referred contractors in healthcare and commercial construction.

The Values That Guide Every Project

From first consultation to final walkthrough, these four principles define how Qamar Construction delivers.

🏆
Uncompromising Quality
We select materials and subcontractors to the same standard we'd want in our own space. Quality isn't something we advertise — it's something every client sees the day they walk in for the first time.
🔍
Full Transparency
No hidden costs. No surprise change orders. Every client gets a detailed fixed-price proposal before we break ground. If something changes, we discuss it openly before a dollar is spent.
📋
True Accountability
We are the single point of contact for your project. When you call, Zain answers. When there's an issue, we solve it. We don't pass responsibility to subs or architects — we own the outcome.
🤝
Community Commitment

Credentials & Expertise

Why Houston Trusts Qamar Construction

Licensed, accredited, insured, and experienced — here's what backs every project we build.

⭐
BBB Accredited Business
Qamar Construction carries an active Better Business Bureau accreditation — a mark that reflects our history of ethical business practices and client dispute resolution.
📜
Texas Licensed General Contractor
We hold all required Texas GC licenses. Every project is built to code, properly permitted, and signed off by the relevant city, county, and state authorities.
🛡️
Fully Insured — General Liability & Workers' Comp
Full general liability coverage and workers' compensation insurance protects both our team and your property on every project. COIs available upon request.
♿
ADA Compliance Specialists
We design and build every commercial and healthcare space to ADA standards — accessible restrooms, doorways, ramps, signage, and parking. Code-compliant from day one.
🏥
TDLR Healthcare Construction Experience
Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation (TDLR) governs healthcare facility construction. We've navigated TDLR permitting on dozens of medical and dental projects.
🔥
Fire Marshal & Health Code Compliance
Restaurants, childcare centers, and medical facilities require specialized fire marshal and health department approvals. Our team manages this process on every applicable project.
