THE MOST CRITICAL PART

We Specialize in the Commercial Kitchen

The kitchen is where restaurant construction either succeeds or fails. Wrong hood placement, undersized grease traps, or improper ventilation will cost you weeks of delays and thousands in corrections. We get it right the first time.

🔥

Hood Systems & Fire Suppression

Type I (grease-laden air) and Type II (heat/moisture) hood sizing, duct routing, makeup air, and Ansul fire suppression system design and installation. Coordinated with your equipment layout before a single wall goes up.

🚿

Grease Trap & Waste Management

Proper grease trap sizing per City of Houston Public Works requirements. Above-ground interceptors for smaller operations; underground vaults for high-volume kitchens. Includes all FOG (fats, oils, grease) plumbing connections.

❄️

Refrigeration & Walk-In Infrastructure

Walk-in cooler and freezer rough-ins, reach-in refrigeration electrical, ice machine plumbing, and cold storage layout coordination with your kitchen equipment vendor.

⚡

Commercial Kitchen Electrical

High-amperage panel sizing for commercial ovens, fryers, combi ovens, and dishwashers. Dedicated circuits per equipment spec, with floor box locations and GFCI protection per NEC commercial code.

🚰

3-Compartment Sink & Handwash

Three-compartment sink plumbing, handsink placement at all required stations per Houston Health Department requirements, floor drain positioning, and mop sink rough-in — all coordinated with your kitchen layout.

🌡️

HVAC & Makeup Air

Commercial kitchen HVAC is dramatically more complex than standard commercial HVAC. We design makeup air units that balance your exhaust hood, prevent negative pressure, and keep your kitchen at a comfortable working temperature.

The commercial kitchen requires specialized knowledge of hood sizing and exhaust requirements, grease trap compliance, high-amperage kitchen electrical, commercial-grade plumbing, and coordination with equipment vendors. Getting any one of these wrong means failed inspections and costly rework.
