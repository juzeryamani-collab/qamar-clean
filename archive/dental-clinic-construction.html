Everything Houston dentists need to know before breaking ground.

A typical dental clinic build-out in Houston takes 10–18 weeks from permit approval to opening day. Permitting alone takes 4–8 weeks in Harris County and surrounding jurisdictions. Qamar Construction manages the full permit process and builds according to your target opening date — we plan every phase backwards from the day you want to open.
Dental office construction in Houston typically ranges from $320,000–$520,000 for a 4-operatory practice and $480,000–$780,000 for a 6-operatory practice. Per square foot costs run approximately $175–$285/sq ft for construction, with renovation of existing space typically 35–55% less than new construction. We provide free custom quotes with full line-item transparency.
Yes — phased dental renovation is one of our specialties. We sequence construction work to keep portions of your practice operational while we build. We schedule noisy and disruptive work during your off-hours and maintain infection control barriers during any clinical work. Many of our dental clients have had zero missed patient days during renovation.
As a turn-key design-build contractor, Qamar Construction handles architectural design and space planning for dental practices as part of our comprehensive service — at no separate architectural fee. We design operatory layouts, sterilization room flow, patient circulation, radiology rooms, and staff zones. If you already have an architect, we coordinate seamlessly.
Building a dental clinic in Houston typically requires: a City of Houston building permit (or county permit for unincorporated areas), mechanical, electrical, and plumbing sub-permits, a Texas DSHS radiology room permit for any X-ray suites, and a final certificate of occupancy for healthcare use. Qamar Construction manages every one of these — you never have to visit a permit office.
