THE MOST CRITICAL ELEMENT

Acoustic Privacy — Your Patient's First Concern

Your patients need to feel safe knowing their sessions are private. Poor acoustic design destroys therapeutic trust. We engineer speech privacy into every square inch of your therapy space.

🔇 STC-Rated Wall Assemblies

Standard walls (STC 35–40) allow speech to be understood in adjacent rooms. We build therapy office walls to STC 50–55 using staggered stud framing, acoustic insulation (Rockwool SafNSound), and resilient channels that decouple drywall from the structure — stopping vibration transmission.

🚪 Acoustic Door Systems

Standard hollow-core doors are acoustically transparent. We specify solid-core acoustic doors with continuous door sweeps and compression seals that achieve STC 40+ when properly installed. Door placement is also designed to prevent direct sound paths between rooms.

📻 Sound Masking Systems

Sound masking introduces a gentle broadband signal (similar to soft HVAC noise) through ceiling speakers that raises the ambient sound level just enough to make speech unintelligible in adjacent spaces. Combined with STC walls, this creates HIPAA-compliant speech privacy that patients notice and trust.

🏗️ Flanking Path Control

Sound travels through flanking paths — plenum spaces above suspended ceilings, HVAC ducts, and electrical chase ways. We extend acoustic walls to the deck above, install duct silencers, and caulk all penetrations to prevent sound flanking around your STC-rated assemblies.

FAQ

Therapy Office Construction Questions

How much does it cost to build a therapy office in Houston?
Mental health office construction costs range widely based on scope. A solo therapy suite (500–1,000 sq ft) typically runs $60,000–$140,000. A small group practice (3–6 rooms, 1,200–2,000 sq ft) runs $150,000–$280,000. Larger counseling centers and behavioral health clinics range from $280,000–$750,000+. Contact us for a free site-specific estimate.
What is the minimum STC rating for therapy office walls?
For therapy and counseling offices, we target STC 50–55 for walls between therapy rooms and adjacent spaces. STC 50 means that a person speaking normally in one room cannot be understood in an adjacent room. This meets HIPAA speech privacy requirements. We achieve this with staggered stud framing, Rockwool acoustic insulation, and resilient channel decoupling — combined with a sound masking system.
Does my therapy office need to be ADA compliant?
Yes. All healthcare and professional offices in Texas must comply with ADA accessibility requirements including accessible parking, compliant entryways, maneuvering clearances at all doors, accessible restrooms, and accessible routes throughout. Qamar Construction handles all ADA compliance design and documentation as part of our permit process.
How long does therapy office construction take in Houston?
A solo therapy suite or small practice build-out typically takes 8–14 weeks from permit approval. Larger counseling centers or behavioral health facilities take 14–22 weeks. Permitting takes 4–6 weeks in Harris County and surrounding areas. We plan your schedule backward from your target opening date.
Can you build a telehealth room as part of my therapy office?
Yes. We build dedicated telehealth rooms with controlled lighting systems, professional backdrop walls, STC-rated acoustic treatment, and structured cabling for camera, microphone, and monitor mounting. A well-designed telehealth room improves the professional quality of remote sessions and patient confidence in your practice.
