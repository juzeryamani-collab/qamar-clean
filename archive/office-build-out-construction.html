Most Houston landlords offer $40–$80/sq ft in tenant improvement allowances for 5+ year leases. We document all scope against your TI, help you negotiate your allowance during lease negotiations, and manage all landlord approval requirements — so you extract full value from every dollar the landlord contributes.

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Office Build-Out Questions

Office build-out costs in Houston range from $65–$85/sq ft for basic tenant improvements to $175–$250+/sq ft for high-end executive suites. A 3,000 sq ft corporate office typically costs $195,000–$525,000 depending on finish level, conference room buildout, glass partitions, and IT infrastructure. Medical and legal offices trend toward the higher end of that range.

A tenant improvement (TI) allowance is money from your landlord toward your build-out, typically expressed as $/sq ft (e.g., $50/sq ft). We help you maximize it by: scoping the full build-out before signing your lease so you know what to negotiate for; documenting all allowable work in a format your landlord approves; and submitting reimbursement requests that satisfy their requirements. We've helped tenants extract hundreds of thousands in TI they almost left on the table.

Timeline depends heavily on scope: a basic TI (new flooring, paint, lighting) takes 4–8 weeks. A standard corporate build-out with conference rooms and full MEP work takes 8–14 weeks. High-end executive suites or complex medical offices take 14–20+ weeks. We compress timelines by submitting permits simultaneously with procurement and keeping you in the loop on every milestone.
