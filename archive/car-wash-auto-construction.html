BUILT-IN TECHNICAL SYSTEMS

The Infrastructure That Makes Your Car Wash Run

💧 Reclaim Water Systems

Car washes consume significant water — reclaim systems capture, settle, and treat wash water for reuse, reducing fresh water consumption by 60–80%. Houston municipal utilities increasingly require reclaim for new commercial car washes. We design and build compliant reclaim tanks, pump stations, and distribution piping as part of our standard scope.

⚡ High-Voltage Electrical

Express tunnel car washes typically require 400–800 amp, 3-phase electrical service. Self-serve and in-bay facilities require 200 amp per bay minimum. We coordinate with CenterPoint Energy for service upgrades, design the electrical infrastructure to match your equipment package, and build in capacity for future expansion.

🧪 Chemical Injection Rough-In

Chemical injection lines supply pre-soak, foam, tire cleaner, and spot-free rinse to precise points in the tunnel. All supply lines, chemical room location, injection stub-ups, and pressure specifications must be coordinated with your equipment vendor before walls and floors are set. Errors are expensive — we get it right the first time.

🏗️ Houston Soil Foundations

Houston's expansive clay soils require engineered foundations for car wash construction — especially given the combination of water exposure, heavy equipment loads, and conveyor vibration. We use geotechnical reports to specify post-tension slabs, piers, or hybrid foundations appropriate for your specific site and car wash format.

🌧️ Site Drainage & Stormwater

Car wash sites produce chemical-laden runoff that must be properly managed. Houston's heavy rainfall events compound the challenge. We design detention and drainage systems that separate clean stormwater from process water, meet city drainage requirements, and protect your operation from flooding — critical in Houston's flat terrain.

🎥 Security & Technology Infrastructure

Modern car washes require significant technology infrastructure: license plate reader conduits, security camera positions, access control, LED lighting systems, and POS/kiosk rough-in. We coordinate all technology rough-ins with your equipment and technology vendors during construction — before walls and ceilings are completed.
